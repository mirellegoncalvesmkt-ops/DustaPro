Frequently Asked Questions

Is it difficult to set up?

Not at all. Just charge it when it arrives, press the single button on it, and it starts cleaning. That’s everything there is to it.

What floors does it work on?

DustaPro works on hard floors and low-pile rugs – wood, tile, laminate, and vinyl. It’s not suited to thick or deep-pile carpet, where a more powerful vacuum would serve you better. For hard floors and low-pile surfaces, it handles daily dust, crumbs, and pet hair consistently well.

What happens when it's done or gets stuck?

When the runtime is complete it stops on its own. If it gets stuck against an obstacle it will reverse and find another direction. You don’t need to monitor it while it runs.

How do I know when it needs charging?

The indicator light on the device will let you know when the battery is running low. Plug it into any USB cable and it’s fully charged within three hours.

Is it powerful enough to actually clean my floor?

DustaPro is built for daily maintenance rather than occasional deep cleans. Run it regularly and dust, crumbs, and pet hair never get a chance to build up, which means the floor stays consistently clean with far less effort than a weekly vacuum.
